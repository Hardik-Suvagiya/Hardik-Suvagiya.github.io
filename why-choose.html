Why Choose Us??

Dental implant

dental implants are like natural replacement of missing teeth. In recent times, Implants are the most successful and comfortable treatment option to get your missing teeth back within a few days to few months

With dental implants, you have NO restrictions on your daily food and you can enjoy your favourite food with almost comfort and joy.

most importantly, when you choose a dental implant, you don't need to go through the treacherous process of teeth trimming because dental implant takes support form bone itself rather than surrounding teeth.

Full mouth Implant therapy

when you have many problems with ill fitting denture, like dislodgement while taking food and talking with others. Dental implant supported ceramic teeth are the best option you can get at Dentè 32.

We are using standardized and World wide recognised brand like Osstem (south Korea) and Computerized (DMLS) ceramic and titanium based teeth for replacement of missing teeth. So you don't need to worry about the quality of your fixed teeth.

Braces therapy

in recent times, the best Smile design is to get your natural teeth into perfect position without artificial cover.

Dentè 32 is the best place to get your treatment planning for braces therapy as Dr. Hardik Suvagiya has mastered his skills for all types of braces therapy like self ligation system (Damon Smile) and clear aligners therapy (Suresmile technology).

Invisible Braces ( clear aligner therapy)

when you don't want to get metal or ceramic braces due to social dilemma, Clear aligners are the best solution to get your perfect smile design.

you don't need to go outside to get your reports for aligner therapy as Denté 32 is equipped with all the machinery in-house.

You can enjoy your smile design journey without any food restriction with clear aligner therapy.

Digital smile design (DSD)

now you can get an idea about your smile design result before commencing treatment through AI based software at Dentè 32.

Digital smile design includes dental veneers and zirconia bridge to get pictures perfect smile at any age after adolescence.

Microscopic Root canal therapy

in Junagadh City, dentè 32 is the only place where you can get Dental Microscope (Labomed USA) for Root canal therapy.

Dr. Hardik Suvagiya has trained for this wonderful technology and upgraded his skill set to save your decayed teeth with utmost precision and perfection in a painless manner.

with this technology, you can get highly precise Root canal therapy with long-term results.

Crown and bridge

for your missing teeth replacement, zirconia bridge is the latest invention in the field of dentistry.

zirconia is the only material with its properties nearer to natural enamel so that you won't feel like mis matched material for your tooth replacement.

zirconia is a tooth coloured material for crown and bridge febrication. You can get very precise tooth cap with CAD-CAM technology using Dental scanner (DENTSPLY SIRONA - Germany) at Dentè 32.

Wisdom tooth surgery

when you don't want to bear a traumatic experience for wisdom tooth removal surgery, contact us for a smooth and total pain free experience as Dr. Hardik Suvagiya and his Dentè 32 team has more than 5000 wisdom tooth removal surgery experience over the period of time.

we use dental 3D x-ray (CBCT) to get complete details of your case before surgery as we have an in-house machine for 3D view (VATECH-South Korea) first time in Junagadh.

Paediatric dentistry

paediatric dental treatment includes, treatment of decayed Milly teeth, treatment for bad habits like thumb sucking and mouth breathing, space maintainer therapy for missing milky teeth, jaw growth modification therapy.

Dentè 32 is committed to provide all pediatrician dental treatment under one roof with a complete solution.

3D Dental x-ray (CBCT)

this is the latest technology for dental x-ray mainly used for diagnosis of complex problems like Dental Implant planning, bone pathology evaluation, impact tooth evaluation, better visualization of root canal system etc.

we are the first dental clinic in Junagadh city to be equipped with this latest machine in-house.
