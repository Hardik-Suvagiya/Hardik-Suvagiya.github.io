Our Team

Our Founder And Director

Dr. Hardik Suvagiya

BDS, MDS Orthodontist, Fellowship in Implantology, PhD continue (Guj. Uni.)

One of Junagadh's own First Goldmedalist MDS Orthodontist Dr. Hardik Suvagiya is providing excellent braces therapy for irregular teeth with high quality equipments like 3M metal and ceramic braces, invisible braces like clear aligner therapy and orthognathic surgery for highly esthetic and cosmetic correction.

He has completed his BDS and MDS from one of the prestigious colleges of Asia, B.J. Medical college Ahmedabad with the highest rank during his academic career.

He has successfully treated around 2000 cases of braces therapy since 2017.

He has also placed more than 1000 dental implants to bring smiles on the faces of geriatric patients.

Dr. Kinnari Suvagiya

Dental Surgeon, Fellowship in Dental Cosmetology

Dr. Kinnari Markana is a very dynamic personality who brings emotional touch with each and every patient while providing dental treatment.

She is providing quality dental treatments like Root canal therapy, crown and bridge, teeth whitening procedure with almost precision and perfection.

She laid a foundation stone of Dentè32 dental and Aesthetic care in 2020 with a unique philosophy in her mind to uplift standards of dental treatment for the people of Junagadh city.

Our Doctor

Dr. Siya Tavethiya (BDS)

Dr. Siya is the most generous personality in dentè 32 team. she is specialised in the field of conservation dentistry. She provides tooth coloured filling for decayed teeth, teeth polishing, dental jewellery and root canal therapy.

Dr. Vrunda Mevcha (BDS)
